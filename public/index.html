How does Belvo protect my data?

The first time you enter your credentials in the application they are sent to Belvo to be stored and encrypted in our servers.

From that moment on, your personal data is transformed into an alphanumeric random code that is impossible to decipher.

This code is used to identify the user without the need to send or expose the content of the credentials at any time.

Each time your application makes a request to obtain your data, our platform connects directly with the financial institution to extract the information without the need to expose its content.

Is it safe to share my credentials with Belvo?

It is completely safe to share your credentials with Belvo.

To protect your privacy and security at all times, we use strong encryption systems that turn your credentials into a unique, random identifier, while your information is kept safe in a private vault.

This identifier is what is used to communicate with the applications, without the need for your private details to be exposed at any time.

How we handle data

How does it work?

To connect your financial data with an app, you only need to enter the data which you normally use to access your financial institution accounts, such as your username and password.
Our platform then encrypts and securely stores this information and safely extracts the data from your account using a read-only access mode, always with your permission, without ever exposing its content.
Once this step is done, our platform creates a secure connection with the app you are using so they can process your financial data and offer you better services.
